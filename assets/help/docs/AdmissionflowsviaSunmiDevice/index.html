Admission flows via Sunmi Device

User needs to enter the credentials to log in to the device.

When you enter you are redirected to a screen to select the outlet name.

The a screen appears where all the flows are visible to the user and you can select one. E.g. if the guest is showing you credit card for the lounge access then select credit card option amongst the nine options.

Admission Token Types

Credit Card
To get airport lounge access customers can bring a credit card that comes with a lounge access feature or lounge access can be at discounted rate .The number of free airport lounge access varies from card to card. Few premium credit cards offer unlimited access to airport lounges along with complimentary guest. click here.

QR Code
To get airport lounge access customers can bring some vouchers or QR-Codes issued by third party. To set up such admission Token. click here

Smart Traveller Voucher
This is a loyality program run by Premium Plaza for members of smart traveller app. ST app API is called to know the membership ID validation. Arrture points can be used to redeem for various rewards.click here

Airline Invitation
Airline Passengers can get complimentary or discounted lounge access or other facilities, depending upon the Airline Program. The Admission Token will be setup by using the boarding pass. click here.

Airline Staff
Airline Crew Members gets complimentary lounge access on the airports .They need to bring the crew ID passes to get lounge access. To set up Airline Staff Token. click here.

Corporate Client Membership
Corporate Partners provides Coupon/Vouchers/Memberships to their customers, they can access complimentary or discounted lounge access and other facilities on the airport. click here

Walk-in
A customer can avail lounge access or other facilities without any credit card or coupon. click here

Online Booking
In this flow guest brings the third party voucher which is generated online and gets lounge access or other facilities with that voucher. Partners like Evoucher, IBE etc generate these vouchers and distribute it to the guests to avail the services.click here

Credit Card Flow:

Click on Credit Card option.

User is then redirected to the next screen where the device asks to enter the following details : Name, Credit card number and its expiry date. Click on Next button once the details are filled.

If more than one type of credit card is following the same BIN then you will see more than one option to choose from (else you will be redirected to the entitlements directly). Here two cards are possessing the same BIN so user needs to choose the one where the image on the device for the card name matches the credit card which guest has shown.

Upon selecting the same, in the next screen all the entitlements are shown to the user which are binded to the credit card. By default the primary entitlement is selected with quantity as '1', ans you can increase the quantity of type of guest which is coming along with the primary card holder (if any). In this screen you will see the terms and conditions also which is very important to be taken care of at the time of admission. Click on 'Next'.

User can now enter the boarding pass details bought by the guest by either scanning the boarding pass or entering the details manually. (Scanning should be preferred). You need to choose one out of 'Use Camera' or 'Manual Entry'.

If 'Manual Entry' was chosen then add the details of the boarding pass .(if three guests are coming along then this process will be repeated thrice). Click on 'Next'.

On the next screen you can recheck the details, click on 'Confirm' if no changes are required else click on back.

If the guest is entitled to have 'Arrture Points' then click on 'Sign in' button else click on 'Confirm'.

If the guest is eligible for 'Arrture points' then you are redirected to this screen where you need to click on 'Scan my membership card'.

Here you get two options either to scan or manually enter the membership id. After entering the membership id click on 'Next button'.

Click on 'Confirm' button.

If any payment is to be paid then this screen appears. Click on the Payment type (Choose either Cash or Bank Terminal and click on 'Confirm'.

If Bank terminal was clicked you need to click on the type of card used for the payment.

Here in this screen, Currency will be auto-selected, you need to enter the total amount to be paid and Payment code (this can be the reference number). Click on 'OK' button.

Once the details are entered, click on 'Confirm'.

In the next screen, take the signature from the guest and click on 'Complete'.

You can now send the receipt to the guest on email id provided by the guest by entering the email address and clicking on send icon on the right side of the field.

Click on 'Print Receipt' or 'Complete'.
