Walkin POS

"Walkin POS" tab is accessible from the sidebar as a "Masters" submenu item. Click on it for a Walkin Admission.

A 'New Walkin Admission' can be done by clicking on 'New' button.

Firstly, you need to select a 'Product'. On this screen only those products are visible which are sellable at the outlet which is selected in the header. The selected product will be highlighted. You can search for a product from the search bar on the top of the screen.

When you select a product then, you need to select the 'Partner', You can see one or more Partners selling the product here with their token names. Select one and proceed.

Once selected, on the right hand side of the screen you will find the details or the order which you can edit. It has PAX and Item fields which you can edit and proceed with payment.

On the bottom left of the screen there is a field to enter the Arrute ID to earn Arrture points, you can input a valid Arrture id and click on 'Validate' button.

Now, for payment of an admission you will see three options namely, Forex, Cash, Card.

For an admission where the guest bring Foreign Currency 'FOREX' mode is to be selected

Select the currency which the guest has bring from the Currency field.

As soon as you select the currency 'Amount' field will be auto-populated with the amount that has to be taken by guest in the forgein currency. Click on 'Add Payment' button.

You can delete the selected payment by clicking on 'Delete' button else clcik on 'Save Payment' button.

After clicking on 'Save Payment' button , under 'Received' field you will see the amount added, which means the payment has been taken.

Click on 'Create Order' button.

The order is created successfully, and the POS screen doesnot close, it remains open for next admission.

Payment by Cash :

Click on 'Cash' button if you have to take cash from the guest for a walkin admission.

As soon as you click on 'CASH' button, on the right hand side of the screen you can see a one liner that the cash is received by 'GSO name'. And under Received field you will see the amount that has been taken by the guest.

Click on 'Create Order' button.

Payment by Card :

Click on 'CARD' button, if the guest wants to pay by Credit/Debit Card.

This screen opens when you click on 'CARD'.

Select/Enter the details like, Payment types, Reference Number, Amount

Click on 'Add Payment' button.

You can delete the payment (if needed) by clicking on delete icon else click on 'Save Payment' button.

Once 'Save Payment' button is clicked, under Received field you can see the received amount.

Click on 'Create Order' button to complete the admission.

When you click on 'Close' icon on the top right side of the screen you will see all create orders.

Note: If you want to undo the steps that you have done for the admission click on 'CANCEL' button. It will revert all the changes done for current admission and will create a blank screen for you to create order.

If any changes are made at the product level e.g. the price of the product then to get it reflected in the Walkin POS screen you need to click on the 'Refresh' button.
