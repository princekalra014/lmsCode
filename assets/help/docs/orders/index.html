Orders

In 'Orders' screen user can see all the admissions done in the outlet with its in-depth details.

"Orders" tab is accessible from the sidebar. Click on it to get an in-depth data view of the admissions.

User can search for the order(s) by entering its details in the search field.

Or, User can click on the dropdown icon for advanced search in which options are present as different fields, use those to filter the search.

Click on "Reset" button to reset the form values.

Click on "Search" button to search for the result.

User can find the orders list in the table.

You can view order's details by double click on that row of the table for whose details you want to see.

System then populates a screen where you can see the complete detail of that particular order including Order Number, Receipt No., Order Amount, etc.

This screen has three horizontal tabs namely, Items, Payments, Boarding Pass.

The Items tab gives a detailed view of products/services availed at or during the admission. Name, SKU, Quantity, Price Tax etc.

Payments tab shows the payment details. Unique Payment ID, Mode & Amount of payment, Collected by, Collect Date etc.

The Boarding Pass tab gives a glimpse of the flight details obtained after scanning or manually entering the boarding pass details of the guest. Each guest will show its unique boarding pass and when scanned while admission the details will be displayed in this tab.

How to add an item to an existing order for an on-going admission.

Once the guest can is admitted to access the lounge, the guest can avail add-on products or services. The new item can be added to the order by GSO. GSO needs to go to 'Order' screen, Double click on the order in which new item has to be added.

Scroll down, under the Items tab and click on 'New Item'.

In product field, enter the product/service name the guest wants to avail. Enter first 3 letters of product/service name the list of options will populate, select one option. Enter the quantity under quantity field. Enter remarks (if applicable).

Click on 'Add Product'.

The details will be displayed below once 'Add Product' is clicked. This is editable, GSO can edit the quantity or delete the product(s) if needed. Click on 'Add Product(s) to order'.

Click on 'Collect Pending Amount' to collect the payment of newly added product/service.

Now the payment details to collect payment will be shown below. Choose one 'Payment Type'. Click on 'Collect Payment' Once the payment has been collected 'Save Payment'.

Click on 'Close' button.

Payment can be collected later as well if the guest wants to pay later. In the order screen the orders with pending payment are highlighted in Red colour. Double click on the order and repeat the above stated procedure to collect payment.

This feature is to settle the payment collected by the GSO.

Click on the Settle button from the top right corner of the 'Order' screen. User (name of the GSO), Select the Date & Time (between which you want to settle the payment).

Click on the search icon next to 'End Time'. It will populate the table showing the payments pending for settlement.

On the left side, checkboxes are given against each payment details (Also, select all is given on the top). Select the payments to be settled.

Click on 'Mark as settled' to settle the payment.

Click on 'Generate PDF' to generate the settlement report in pdf format to further reference.

Click on the 'Close' button to exit the screen.
