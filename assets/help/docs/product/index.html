Product

You can add new products manually or import complete file containing list of products and their details. Also, edit existing product's details in the system for futher use.

Process to Import the Products

"Products" tab is accessiable from the sidebar as a "Masters" submenu item. Click on it to get in-depth data view of all the products.

You can search for the product(s) by entering its details in the search field.

Or, you can click on dropdown icon for advance serach in which you are having more option to filter out list.

Click on "Reset" button to reset the form values.

Click on "Search" button to search for the result.

You can find products list in the table.

You can edit/delete the particular product from the system by clicking on the respective icons present in the "Action" column of the table.

If you have clicked on delete(trash) icon then it will open the popup for you, where you have to click on "Yes' button if you are sure about deleting. Else, click on the "No" button.

If you have clicked on edit icon then you can see the details of that particular contact. Or, you can double click on the row of the table to view/edit particular product information.

System then populates a screen where you can see the complete detail of that particular product including product name, type, currency, loyalty, description, etc.

Also, you can see one horizontal tab in the details page below the form fields - Products Available At.

By default "Products Available At" data is visible, where you can see the which particular product is available like the names and other details of the outlet where the products is available.

You can see the available product details and other information in the table.

You can also add new product for multiple outlets at a time by clicking on "Add Product at Outlet" button present on the top right of the table.

In this screen, you can fill the required details to add new product.

In "Lounge Name" field you can add multiple lounges at a time from the available option in the dropdown.

Or, click on "Select Lounge" button to select multiple lounge after seeing their details.

It will open a popup for you where you can see the lounges name in table.

Then click on "Search Field" button to search the required data in the table.

You can select multiple lounges at a time for the product.

As shown below the selected available outlet will move to selected outlet with the help of arrows.

Click on "Close" button to close the popup.

Click on "Reset" button to reset the form.

Click on "Add Product At Outlet" to save the details.

New outlet added in product is shown below in table .

Click on Product Partner link Tab.

Click on Add Product Partner link button.

Fill the partner name and Partner Product ID .

Click on Reset to reset the edited fields.

To save the fields click on Add Partner Product button

Click on "Localisation" tab to view the data in different languages.

You can find the table for existing attributes.

Click on "Save" to save the data.

Clicking on submit button will bring the user to the outlet screen

You can also add new product by clicking on "New" button present on the top right corner on the main screen.

Fill the following information to add new product in the system.

Click on "Reset" button to reset the form.

Click on "Save" button to save the filled details in the system.

Click on "Import" button so that you can import the complete file having required data in one go.

Follow the steps present in "Import" tab present the sidenav of this documentation.

Below are the input fields used in this screen:

Name of the field Entry Mode Mandatory Comments
Product Name Free text with dropdown Yes You have to enter starting three alphabets of the product name and it will show you the result based on that. Once you have selected any product from the dropdown, you will see some of the fields get auto filled.
Product Sku Free Text Yes Write the specific short code for that product
Product Type Selection Yes System generated once you select product name. Also, you can fill it yourself. Type could have following values - Goods & Services.
Product Category Selection Yes System generated once you select product name. Also, you can fill it yourself. Category could have following values - Convenience, Food & Beverages, Health & Wellness, Lounge Access, Retail.
Currency Selection Yes System generated once you select product name. Also, you can fill it yourself. Currency could have following values - USD, YEN etc.
Product Price Number Yes System generated once you select product name. Also, you can fill it yourself. You can also change the price of the product by entering its current amount.
Loyalty Points Number Yes System generated once you select product name. Also, you can fill it yourself. You can give loyalty point on purchase/take on each product or services.
Applicable Tax Free Text with dropdown Yes System generated once you select product name. Also, you can fill it yourself. User can change the tax for each product, First need to be defined in Tax Screen then here you can select from dropdown and select the specific tax name

Outlet Name Selection(Multiple value) Yes You can select multiple outlets at a time to make the product available at.

Product Partner Link Tab :

This tab has two fields Partner Name and Partner Product I'd. In partner field we enter the name of the partner and in Partner Product we enter the I'd provided by the partner.
Partner Product ID : A product Id is given by the partner through this Id we bind the product name in MAS 3.0 Eg a partner A has a product lounge access whose id is SV001 then to bind the product in MAS 3.0 we give the same Id in this field.

Localisation Tab :

This field displays the name of admission token/ outlet/ product in a specific language eg Chinese and when the language from the header is to Chinese then on the respective screen the name will be displayed in Chinese
